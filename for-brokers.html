Where I’m the right call — and where I’m not.

Honest fit signals save us both a wasted call.

Strong fit

  • A programme that’s red, stalled or politically stuck.
  • Regulated, production-critical environments (pharma, finance, public).
  • Datacenter / mainframe / cloud transition and migration.
  • Carve-out, separation or TSA exit under a fixed date.
  • NIS2 / DORA / GxP that needs to move from paper to operating.
  • Multi-vendor, multi-country governance that has broken down.

Probably not me

  • Pure hands-on software development or architecture roles.
  • Greenfield start-ups with no regulatory or governance dimension.
  • Junior or PMO-analyst-level coordination.
  • Permanent-only mandates (I work interim / freelance).
  • Sales / pre-sales or account management.
